Have you ever jerked off (yes both of you guy or gal) if you have then you know, How when you complete you are both satisfied and feel guilty. That’s what happens when you read too much about a particular topic which you are trying to improve upon.

I call this Knowledge Mastrubation (I did not coin the term.I am borrowing it. I read it in one of the 2 “dating playbooks” I bought. If this blog goes viral I promise I will mention the creator’s name and his gumroad link). Just like mastrubation is not linked with sex. Knowledge Mastrubation is not linked with actual accomplishment. It boils down to theory and practical in the end. This is it this is the point. When you read a book you need to ask yourself a very important question, Why am I reading this? If this question doesn’t pop up when you pick up that stack of papers. You are doing a disservice to yourself and you are probably better of not reading that book at all. There are so many Books I started reading and never finished. So many that I got the gist and just stopped midway since I could sense I cannot derive much from it anymore so it doesn’t make sense for me to continue.

After years of reading I realized it’s better to stop reading if the book is not interesting than to read it till completion. It feels like excessive mastrubation. That OCD of reading the last page when you hate what you are reading will drive you crazy. Sleepless nights and headaches are confirmed. And if you are weak then you might also end up puking. Don’t read if you hate it. So you read the kindle sample finished it bought the book on AMAZON and now it doesn’t hold up the same attention like that sample did. Cool chuck it its not worth your time, Its okay you wasted your money on it. But if you have netflix and pay for it monthly you already are okay with wasting money. Those 6$ were still well spent on that book. You bought that hard cover after reading the first 2 pages of it from Crossword now the next chapter feels like someone is trying to sexually assault you. Don’t worry, try to return the book, if not give it to the rag picker. Or keep it like a trophy to show off. Whatever you do just DON’T READ that garbage. Don’t put your mind through that torture. It is already tired with all that excessive jerking off. Let me let you in on a industry trade secret. Authors are told by publishers to have “killer openings” simply for this reason. So that after reading a few pages you buy the book. A book at the end of the day is just like another product. It needs to sell to make money. Next time you feel “tricked” into buying a book. It’s okay you are dumb.It’s okay to be dumb sometimes. The system works because of dumb people like you and me.

This is the attention economy now. Who gets the most eye balls makes the most money. The older times when a book was supposed to be the “Knowledge of the world” is gone. Now every book has to compete with an iPAD with PUBG installed in it. That book is no match to the social media app(whichever you prefer, insta, twitter, snapchat, YT) on your phone. Books don’t add value to your life like they used to. Most of it recycled garbage. Unless you are an experienced reader it would be next to impossible for you to differentiate between a good book to a bad one. So stop holding them up on a pedestal. It was written by a human like you and me.

YOU CAN TAKE ACTION without ANY KNOWLEDGE about that thing you want to improve at all.

Take Action get the ball moving. Then Add reading as a way to accentuate your Actions. That is one of the ways I found some improvement in a few things I am struggling right now.

And not every book deserves your attention. Sometimes a book might look attractive from outside or for a few pages might appear smart and funny. But as you get to know it better you realise what load of crap it is. In that case its ALWAYS better throw it away from your life. Rather than letting it ruin your peace

Throw that book away.
