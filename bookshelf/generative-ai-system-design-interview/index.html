    Notes / Summary for the Book

    Generative AI System Design Interview (Full Colour Edition) by Alex Xu, Ali Aminian, Hao Sheng

    This book provides an insider’s perspective on what interviewers are truly looking for in GenAI system design interviews and why. It includes:

    • A 7-step framework to help tackle GenAI system design interview questions
    • 10 real-world GenAI system design questions with in-depth solutions
    • 280+ diagrams to demystify complex GenAI systems

    The book is particularly helpful for:

    • Software engineers preparing for GenAI system design interviews at major tech companies
    • AI/ML professionals looking to understand production-scale generative AI systems
    • System designers working with large language models and generative AI applications

    Key Topics Covered:

    • GenAI system design fundamentals
    • Large-scale AI system architecture patterns
    • Real-world GenAI system examples (ChatGPT, Google Translate, etc.)
    • Interview preparation strategies and frameworks
    • Text-to-image, text-to-video, and multimodal AI systems
